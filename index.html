The St. Joseph's Church and Parochial School in Hays, Kansas is a historic church and school at 210 W. 13th and 217 W. 13th. They were added to the National Register in 2008.Listed are the St. Joseph's Church and the St. Joseph's Parochial School across the street, but not another school building.

School Library

Founded in 1860

Lawrence College Ghora Gali is a school situated in Murree Tehsil, Punjab, Pakistan. The College is located in the foothills of the Himalayas and Pir Panjal at a height of about 1950 metres (6395 feet) above sea level, covering an area of 150 acres (61 ha). It is 4 km from Murree and 57 km from Rawalpindi/Islamabad.
